GSC23-Bangkok Sponsorship Opportunities:

Bronze Sponsor

Bronze ($1,000) sponsors will receive the following benefits:

  1. Acknowledgment of your sponsorship on the GSC (www.gensc.org), including links to your company’s website.
  2. Inclusion of the sponsor’s logo in meeting materials.
Silver Sponsor

Silver ($2,500) sponsors will receive the following benefits:

  1. Acknowledgment of your sponsorship on the GSC (www.gensc.org), including links to your company’s website.
  2. Inclusion of the sponsor’s logo in meeting materials
  3. One free meeting attendee
  4. Signage at meeting
Gold Sponsor

Gold ($5,000) sponsors will receive the following benefits:

  1. Acknowledgment of your sponsorship on the GSC (www.gensc.org), including links to your company’s website.
  2. Inclusion of the sponsor’s logo in meeting materials
  3. One free meeting registration
  4. Signage at meeting
  5. Table top exhibition display
  6. Half page advertisement in meeting literature
  7. Organization name and logo recognition on meeting signage
Pineapple Platinum Sponsor

Platinum Sponsor ($9,950) will receive the following benefits:

  1. Acknowledgment of your sponsorship on the GSC (www.gensc.org), including links to your company’s website
  2. Inclusion of the sponsor’s logo in meeting materials
  3. Two free meeting registrations
  4. Signage at meeting.
  5. Table top exhibition display
  6. Poster Presentation
  7. Organization name and logo recognition on meeting signage
  8. Guaranteed 20 minute speaking slot in the program of the annual GSC meeting (or two 10 min slots)
  9. Special recognition of Platinum sponsor at symposium and in print and marketing materials
  10. Opportunity to introduce keynote speaker
  11. Full-page symposium program advertisement
  12. Sponsor representative to join GSC board for GSC22 board dinner
