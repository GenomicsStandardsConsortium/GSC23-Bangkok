GSC23-Bangkok Sponsorship Opportunities:

Bronze Sponsor

Bronze ($1,000) sponsors will receive the following benefits:

  1. Acknowledgment of your sponsorship on the meeting website, including links to your company’s website.
  2. Inclusion of the sponsor’s logo in meeting materials.
Silver Sponsor

Silver ($2,500) sponsors will receive the following benefits:

  1. Acknowledgment of your sponsorship on the meeting website, including links to your company’s website.
  2. Inclusion of the sponsor’s logo in meeting materials.
  3. One free meeting registration.
  4. Organization name and logo recognition on meeting signage.
  5. Logo included on the inter-session slides.
Gold Sponsor

Gold ($5,000) sponsors will receive the following benefits:

  1. Acknowledgment of your sponsorship on the meeting website, including links to your company’s website.
  2. Inclusion of the sponsor’s logo in meeting materials.
  3. One free meeting registration.
  4. Table top exhibition displaydisplay (~1.6 m, 5ft).
  5. Half page advertisement in meeting literature.
  6. Organization name and logo recognition on meeting signage.
  7. Logo included on the inter-session slides.
Platinum Sponsor

Platinum Sponsor ($9,950) will receive the following benefits:

  1. Guaranteed 15-minute speaking slot in the program of the annual GSC meeting.
  2. Acknowledgment of your sponsorship on the meeting website, including links to your company’s website
  3. Inclusion of the sponsor’s logo in meeting materials.
  4. Two free meeting registrations.
  5. Choice of full booth exhibition display (3m/10ft) or table top display (~1.6m, 5ft).
  6. Organization name and logo recognition on meeting signage
  7. Logo included on the inter-session slides.
  8. Special recognition of Platinum sponsor at symposium and in print and marketing materials.
  9. Full-page symposium program advertisement.
  10. Sponsor representative to join GSC board for GSC23 board dinner.
